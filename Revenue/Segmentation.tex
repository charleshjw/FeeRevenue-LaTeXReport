% 
% Segment business fee revenues into mutually exclusive revenues, each of which
% have distinctive risk profile. We will present a few available revenue
% segmentation below. Here are some questions help to start the process:
% \begin{itemize}
%   \item Given risk assessment results, how should we breakdown the \BU\ fee
%   revenue?
%   \item What non-financial business drivers are associated with revenues? Should
%   we build model on business drivers or on revenue?
%   \item What segmentation of revenue is satisfactory based on statistical
%   robustness and business intuition?
% %   \item Historical Data: Investigate available revenue and business drivers
% %   data sources and historical time periods. Does available data include stress period that reflect risks identified by risk assessment? 
%   \item Does business experence a regime change such as merger\&acquisition? The
%   model should build on time periods of the same business regime.
%   \item Is there any extraordinary events and outliers that need
%   to be adjusted?
% \end{itemize}
% 
% Revenue data is usually available at management unit level or at
% product\/service level. At least 2 segmentation system will be discussed
% below.\\
% 
% The historical trend of revenues and the cross correlation of revenue against
% external macroeconomic variables are shown in the appendix.
